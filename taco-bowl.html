Taco Bowl

Description

A nice recipe for taco bowls

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • Half an onion
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Crushed corn chips
  • Sliced olivees
  • shredded cheese
  • Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan over medium heat
  2. Add meat and brown
  3. Drain meat and add seasoning with water
  4. Put onion, lettuce, tomato, chips, and olives in a bowl
  5. Add meat and top with cheese
