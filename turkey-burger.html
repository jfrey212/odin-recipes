Turkey Burger

Description

This is a recipe for nice turkey burgers

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground turkey
  • Chiapetti Seasoning
  • Safflower oil

Steps

  1. Form ground turkey into patties and season both sides with Chiapetti
  2. Coat pan with oil and preheat over medium heat
  3. Add turkey patties to pan
  4. Flip once bottom side is browned
  5. Remove from pan when patties read 165 degrees F
